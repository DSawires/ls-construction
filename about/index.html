A Legacy of Craftsmanship

Life Style was founded with a singular vision: to create furniture that embodies the perfect balance of form and function. What began as a small workshop has grown into a leading name in bespoke furniture, serving discerning clients across 25 countries.

Our journey started in Egypt, where ancient traditions of woodworking and craftsmanship have been passed down through generations. We honor this heritage while embracing contemporary design principles, creating pieces that are both timeless and modern.

Today, Life Style stands as a testament to what's possible when passion meets precision. Every piece that leaves our workshop carries with it the dedication of our master artisans and the promise of enduring quality.
